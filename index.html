For this year's Make It Glow contest, we are giving away huge prize bundles! Do you have a great idea about a new kind of bike light? Fabric that glows under black-light? Post an Instructable about anything that lights up, and shines in the dark (or the light!), for your chance to win Canon Cameras, Arduinos, black-lights, and more!

The rule for the contest is simple: any Instructable that demonstrates an original idea or project about light, or illumination, is eligible. What amazing project are you going to illuminate Instructables with? Good luck, we can't wait to see what you are going to make.

Multiple entries are accepted, but each entrant can only win one prize. Contest is open to entries from US, Canada [excluding Quebec], UK, China, Belgium, the Netherlands, Australia, Colombia, Germany, Switzerland, Norway, India, New Zealand, and Denmark. The Contest begins at 12:00 a.m. Pacific Standard Time (PT), October 21, 2013. Contest closes for entries at 11:59pm PT, January 6, 2014.
